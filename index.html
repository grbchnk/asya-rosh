FAQ

For many people, transitioning to intuitive eating ends in overeating and relapses. They end up giving up on the idea, thinking that IE doesn't work.

In reality, there can be several reasons: you haven't addressed food addiction, you don't know how to cope with emotions without the help of food, you have an unbalanced diet, you haven't gotten rid of the diet mentality, etc.

That's why you need the help and support I provide in my projects to make a successful transition to intuitive eating.

The Intensive is NOT a substitute for psychotherapy and treatment, so the training can only be supplementary material in your healing process.

If you need help in finding a specialist, we would be happy to recommend one of the excellent psychotherapists on our team.

On the "Light" tariff, the marathon materials will remain with you for 1 month after completing the course. On the other tariffs, it is 2 months.

No, there are no diets, restrictions, or workouts in the marathon. You will gain knowledge that will help you customize your diet and activity based on your own preferences and needs.

Yes, the Intensive program is designed to overcome overeating and reconnect with your body, allowing you to regain a healthy weight or maintain it without much effort.
